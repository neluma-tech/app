Privacy Policy - English Turkish Dictionary

Last Update: March 3, 2025

Thank you for using English Turkish Dictionary ("the App"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app and services.

1. Definitions

  • App: Refers to the English Turkish Dictionary application.
  • User: Any individual using the App.
  • Preferences: User-defined settings within the App, such as night mode, font size, and voice speed.
  • Personal Data: Any information that could be used to identify an individual. The App does not collect any personal data.

2. Information Collection

The English Turkish Dictionary app does not collect or store any personal data from users. All user preferences are stored locally on the user's device and are not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not use or process user information in any way. The app operates entirely offline for most features, ensuring your data remains private on your own device.

4. Information Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third parties. Since no data is collected, there is no data to share.

5. Data Retention

As we do not collect or retain any personal data, there is no retention policy necessary. User preferences remain only on their own devices.

6. Security

The app is designed with user privacy in mind. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, risks related to data breaches are minimized.

7. Your Choices

All preferences and settings are managed locally on your device. You can reset or modify your settings at any time within the app.

8. Minors

The English Turkish Dictionary app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect any personal data, there is no risk of exposing minors' data.

9. Future Advertising Policy

Currently, the English Turkish Dictionary app is ad-free, providing users with a seamless experience. However, to support the continuous development and maintenance of the app, we may introduce advertisements in future updates. These ads will be carefully selected to match user preferences and will be age-appropriate, ensuring that the user experience remains uncompromised. Any changes to our advertising practices will be reflected in updates to this privacy policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at neluma.tech@outlook.com.

By using the App, you agree to this privacy policy. We may update this policy from time to time, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
